Team Transactions

6/22/2023 The Pend Oreille Wampus Cats trade 2B Jeter Downs and the Middletown Legg Hounds 2024 4th round draft pick to the Missouri Royals in exchange for OF Matt Vierling .
6/14/2023 The Pend Oreille Wampus Cats trade P Adam Ottavino to the Middletown Legg Hounds in exchange for P Diego Castillo and a 2024 4th round pick.
6/12/2023 The Pend Oreille Wampus Cats trade 3B Justin Turner to the Middletown Legg Hounds in exchange for P Trevor Rogers.
6/2/2023 The Pend Oreille Wampus Cats trade OF Hunter Renfroe, C Garrett Stubbs and P Wily Peralta to the Tampa Bay Pipers in exchange for P Bryce Elder , C Joey Bart, and OF Ben Gamel.
2/2/2023 The Pend Oreille Wampus Cats add OF Skye Bolt, and P Jackson Kowar and released OF Yermin Mercedes and P Max Kranick in Phase III of the TRABL Draft.
1/24/2023 The Pend Oreille Wampus Cats cut P Brett Anderson, P Wade LeBlanc, P Richard Rodriguez, P Sam Clay, P Craig Stammen, P Adonis Medina, P Luke Jackson, P John Curtiss, P Yerry Rodriguez, OF Nick Martini, OF Josh Reddick, 2B Jose Peraza, OF Ka'ai Tom, 3B Phil Gosselin, C Cam Gallagher and SS Lucius Fox.
12/19/2022 The Pend Oreille Wampus Cats trade P Jacob deGrom and P Patrick Corbin to the Springfield Schnauzers in exchange for 2B Jonathan India, P Aaron Ashby, and P Mitch Keller.
12/06/2022 The Pend Oreille Wampus Cats trade OF Corey Dickerson to the Little Compton Little Giants in exchange for the 2023 9th & 11th round draft picks.
11/18/2022 The Pend Oreille Wampus Cats trade 3B Max Muncy to the Seattle Rainiers in exchange for SS Amed Rosario.
11/14/2022 Jared Horton assumes ownership of the Philly Cheesesteaks and renames the franchise the Pend Oreille Wampus Cats.
