Team Transactions

2/2/2023 The Pend Oreille Wampus Cats add OF Skye Bolt, and P Jackson Kowar and released OF Yermin Mercedes and P Max Kranick in Phase III of the TRABL Draft.
1/24/2023 The Pend Oreille Wampus Cats cut P Brett Anderson, P Wade LeBlanc, P Richard Rodriguez, P Sam Clay, P Craig Stammen, P Adonis Medina, P Luke Jackson, P John Curtiss, OF Nick Martini, OF Josh Reddick, 2B Jose Peraza, OF Ka'ai Tom, 3B Phil Gosselin, C Cam Gallagher and SS Lucius Fox.
12/19/2022 The Pend Oreille Wampus Cats trade SP Jacob deGrom and SP Patrick Corbin to the Springfield Schnauzers in exchange for 2B Jonathan India, SP Aaron Ashby, and SP Mitch Keller.
12/06/2022 The Pend Oreille Wampus Cats trade Corey Dicerson to the Little Compton Little Giants in exchange for the 2023 9th & 11th round draft picks.
11/18/2022 The Pend Oreille Wampus Cats trade 3B Max Muncy to the Seattle Rainiers in exchange for SS Amed Rosario.
11/14/2022 Jared Horton assumes ownership of the Philly Cheesesteaks and renames the franchise the Pend Oreille Wampus Cats.
